About Us

The National AIDS, Viral Hepatitis and STIs Control Programme (NASCP)
is a division of the Department of Public Health, in the Nigeria Federal Ministry of Health (FMH).
1
Vision, Mission and Mandate

An AIDS-free Nigeria with zero new infections, zero stigma & discrimination, and zero AIDS-related deaths.

2
Brief
Health-sector leadership for Nigeria’s
response to HIV, viral hepatitis and STIs.
3
Work With Us
Partner with NASCP to strengthen HIV, hepatitis & STI services.
Explore careers, consultancies, grants and collaboration opportunities.
